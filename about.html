About

This website's whole goal is to bring people that are interested in Computer Science and unite as a whole. Not only that, but also give an opportunity for other people to see what the Computer Science world is like and join the community! Give it a try and see what the Computer Science field has to offer.


What is needed to join?

Anyone can join the community. Do not worry if you do not possess the necessary skills in order to participate Computer Science tasks. Most believe mathematical skills are needed the most in order to be successful in the Computer Science field. However, and article with the name of “Unravelling the Numerical and Spatial Underpinnings of Computational Thinking: a Pre-Registered Replication Study.” proves otherwise with evidence given. This article is from a peer-reviewed academic journal: give it a read if interested!


Which path to start?

Start by picking which path seems interesting to you! Instead of starting from the beginning learning simple Computer Science, try starting from your chosen path and goal. I got this interesting idea from the article, “The Case for Alternative Endpoints in Computing Education."" which is also from a peer-reviewed journal. Read this one as well if you are intersted!
