Which skills are important for accountatns?

This article is from a peer-reviewed journal by the name of Journal of Education for Business. This journal contains research-based articles that primarly focus on entrepreneurship success. The chosen subject for this article is accounting and which computer skills are needed the most. There were six computer skills that were being analized: accounting software, databases, e-mail or internet, programming, spreadsheets, and word processing. Longtitudinal analysis is provided, as well with responses from actual professional accountants. Results are compared from years ranging from 2001-2009. Results were that all computer skills are important except programming.
